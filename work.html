Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the entire design process take?

The complete process typically takes 10-20 weeks depending on project complexity. Residential projects average 12 weeks, while commercial spaces may take 16-20 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation.

Do I need to be present during the entire process?

Not at all! We handle everything and provide regular updates. We only need your presence for key decisions, approvals, and the final handover. Our project manager will coordinate everything seamlessly.

How do you handle changes during the project?

We have a structured change management process. Minor adjustments can be accommodated easily, while major changes may require design revisions and timeline adjustments. We maintain transparent communication about any impacts.

What happens if there are delays?

We build buffer time into our schedules and proactively manage risks. In case of unavoidable delays, we immediately communicate the situation and revised timeline. Our track record shows 95% on-time delivery.

Do you provide warranty for your work?

Yes! We offer a comprehensive 2-year warranty on all workmanship and 1-year on materials. We also provide lifetime support for any queries or guidance you may need after project completion.
