Our Story

Discover the passion, expertise, and vision behind Smartspace Living

Crafting Spaces Since 2010

Founded by award-winning designer Anika Sharma, Smartspace Living emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary living experiences that reflect the unique personalities and lifestyles of our clients.

What began as a small design studio in Mumbai has grown into one of India's most sought-after interior design firms, with a portfolio spanning luxury residences, commercial spaces, and hospitality projects across the country.

Our philosophy centers on the belief that exceptional design should be both beautiful and functional, creating environments that inspire, comfort, and elevate everyday life.
