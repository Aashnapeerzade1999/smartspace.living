Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our interior design services and process

Design Process

What is your design process?

Our design process follows these key stages:

  • Initial Consultation: Understanding your requirements, budget, and vision
  • Site Measurement: Detailed measurement and analysis of your space
  • Concept Development: Creating initial design concepts and mood boards
  • Design Presentation: Presenting 3D visualizations and material samples
  • Finalization: Refining designs based on your feedback
  • Execution: Project management and quality control during implementation
  • Handover: Final walkthrough and project completion
Do you provide 3D design visualizations?

Yes, we provide detailed 3D visualizations for all our projects. This includes:

  • Photorealistic 3D renders of each room
  • Virtual walkthroughs of the entire space
  • Multiple angle views for better understanding
  • Material and color variations
  • Furniture layout simulations

These visualizations help you see exactly how your space will look before we begin execution.

Can I be involved in the design process?

Absolutely! We believe in collaborative design. Your involvement includes:

  • Regular design review meetings
  • Feedback sessions at every major milestone
  • Material and finish selection participation
  • Site visits during execution
  • Final approval before each phase begins

We consider you an essential part of our design team.

Pricing & Payments

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing is transparent and structured as follows:

  • Design Fee: 10-15% of project cost (includes concepts, drawings, project management)
  • Material Cost: Actual cost of materials with no markup
  • Labor Cost: Based on project complexity and duration
  • Payment Schedule: 40% advance, 40% during execution, 20% on completion

We provide detailed quotations with complete cost breakdowns.

Are there any hidden costs?

No, we maintain complete transparency in our pricing. All potential costs are discussed upfront, including:

  • Government taxes and duties
  • Permit and approval fees
  • Site preparation costs
  • Material transportation charges
  • Waste disposal fees

Any additional costs arising during the project are communicated immediately and require your approval.

Do you offer EMI or payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options:

  • Standard Payment Plan: 40-40-20 as per project milestones
  • Extended Payment Plan: Monthly installments over 3-6 months
  • Bank Financing: Partnership with leading banks for home improvement loans
  • Credit Card: EMI options available through partner banks

We can customize payment plans based on your financial comfort.

Project Timeline

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity:

  • 1 BHK Apartment: 4-6 weeks
  • 2 BHK Apartment: 6-8 weeks
  • 3 BHK Apartment: 8-10 weeks
  • Villa/Bungalow: 12-16 weeks
  • Office Space: 6-10 weeks
  • Commercial Retail: 8-12 weeks

These are estimated timelines and may vary based on specific requirements.

What factors can affect the project timeline?

Several factors can impact project duration:

  • Client Decisions: Timely approvals and material selections
  • Material Availability: Imported or custom materials may have longer lead times
  • Site Conditions: Structural issues or unexpected repairs
  • Weather Conditions: For projects involving exterior work
  • Government Approvals: Permit processing times
  • Festival Seasons: Labor availability during major holidays
Can I stay in my home during renovation?

This depends on the scope of work:

  • Partial Renovation: Usually possible with proper planning
  • Complete Home Makeover: Recommended to arrange alternative accommodation
  • Kitchen/Bathroom Renovation: Temporary kitchen setup can be arranged
  • Dust and Noise: We implement dust containment systems

We assess each situation and provide recommendations for your comfort and safety.

Materials & Quality

What quality of materials do you use?

We use premium quality materials from trusted brands:

  • Plywood: Greenply, Centuryply, or equivalent BWR grade
  • Hardware: Hettich, Hafele, or equivalent premium brands
  • Paints: Asian Paints, Berger, or Dulux premium range
  • Sanitaryware: Jaquar, Kohler, or equivalent
  • Flooring: Kajaria, Somany, or imported options
  • Lighting: Philips, Wipro, or specialized lighting brands

All materials come with manufacturer warranties.

Do you provide material warranties?

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranties:

  • Workmanship Warranty: 12 months on all executed work
  • Material Warranty: As per manufacturer terms (typically 5-25 years)
  • Annual Maintenance: Optional AMC available
  • Emergency Support: 24/7 support for critical issues

All warranty details are clearly mentioned in our contract.

Can I source my own materials?

While we recommend using our sourcing for quality assurance, you can source materials with these conditions:

  • Materials must meet our quality standards
  • We need to approve technical specifications
  • You handle delivery and storage
  • Material warranties become your responsibility
  • Project timeline may be affected by delivery delays

We'll guide you on specifications if you choose to source materials.
