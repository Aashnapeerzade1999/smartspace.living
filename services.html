Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical interior design project take?

The timeline varies based on project scope and complexity. A standard residential project typically takes 8-12 weeks from concept to completion, while larger commercial projects may take 3-6 months. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work within a specific budget?

Absolutely! We believe great design should be accessible at various budget levels. We work closely with you to understand your budget constraints and create a design plan that maximizes value while delivering exceptional results.

What is included in your design services?

Our comprehensive services include initial consultation, space planning, concept development, 3D visualizations, material selection, furniture curation, project management, and installation oversight. We handle every aspect of your project from start to finish.

Do you work with existing furniture or only new pieces?

We can incorporate existing furniture that you love while supplementing with new pieces to complete the look. We'll assess your current items and provide recommendations on what to keep, refurbish, or replace.
