Our Story

Founded in 2012 by visionary interior designer Ananya Sharma, Smartspace Living began as a small studio with a big dream: to revolutionize interior design in India by blending traditional craftsmanship with contemporary aesthetics.

What started as a passion project in a Mumbai apartment has grown into one of India's most trusted interior design firms, with projects spanning across 25+ cities and a team of 50+ creative professionals.

Our journey has been guided by a simple philosophy: every space tells a story, and we're here to help you write yours. From cozy apartments to luxury villas, corporate offices to boutique hotels, we've had the privilege of transforming over 500 spaces into beautiful, functional environments.
