Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical interior design project take?

The timeline varies based on project scope and complexity. A standard residential project typically takes 8-12 weeks from concept to completion, while larger commercial projects may take 3-6 months. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work within a specific budget?

Absolutely! We believe great design should be accessible at various budget levels. We work closely with you to understand your budget constraints and create a design plan that maximizes value while delivering exceptional results.

What areas do you serve?

While our primary studio is in Mumbai, we undertake projects across India. We've successfully completed residential and commercial projects in Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, and other major cities. International projects are considered on a case-by-case basis.

Do you provide 3D visualizations?

Yes, we create detailed 3D visualizations for all our projects. This allows you to virtually walk through your space before implementation begins, ensuring the final result matches your vision perfectly.

What is your design process?

Our process includes: Initial consultation, concept development, detailed design planning, 3D visualization, material selection, project management, and final installation. We maintain transparent communication throughout every stage.
